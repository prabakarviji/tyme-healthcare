Why?

Data-Driven Early Detection

Empowering healthcare providers with the data they need to make informed, timely decisions — because cancer doesn’t wait.

Cancer is one of the most pressing health challenges of our time. In the United States alone, 2,041,910 new cancer cases are projected to be diagnosed in 2025, with 618,120 cancer-related deaths. With 1 in 2 people likely to develop some form of cancer during their lifetime, the need for early detection and proactive care has never been more critical.

TyMe Institute platform provides healthcare providers with the tools they need to make timely, informed decisions that can improve patient outcomes and save lives.

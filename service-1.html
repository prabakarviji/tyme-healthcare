About TyMe Screening

At TyMe, we’re transforming the way cancer is detected—early, accurately, and proactively. Our advanced screening services use a powerful blend of blood-based biomarkers, AI-assisted imaging, genomic insights, and immune profiling to uncover risks before symptoms emerge.

Our Commitment

We’re on a mission to detect cancer at its earliest and most treatable stages, using personalized screening strategies rooted in precision diagnostics and continuous monitoring.

The TyMe Vision

We aim to redefine early detection by combining cutting-edge technology with individualized risk assessments, empowering you to take control of your health—before cancer advances.

TyMe Cancer Screening is ideal for

  • Individuals with a family history of cancer
  • People who value proactive health management
  • Anyone seeking peace of mind through modern, preventive care

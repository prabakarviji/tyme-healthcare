A Guidance to Better Your Health

About TyMe Prevention

It is designed for those who believe in taking control of their health—before cancer becomes a threat. By focusing on what you can influence today—your lifestyle, immune system, and metabolism—we help you build long-term resilience against cancer.

Our Purpose

We’re here to reduce your lifetime cancer risk through personalized, science-based strategies that optimize your body’s defenses—from immunity to metabolism to daily habits.

A New Vision for Health

Our goal is bold to shift healthcare from reactive treatment to prevention-first living—where resilience, not disease, defines your health journey.

Our Cancer Prevention Program offers

A dedicated Health Guide—you are paired with a personal coach to help you implement, adapt, and evolve your prevention plan over time. Your program is powered by insights from

  • Your screening results
  • Your TyMe BioVault data
  • Your immune and metabolic profile
  • This isn’t a one-time intervention—it’s a living strategy.

Our Prevention Program Includes

Nutrition Guidance

Anti-inflammatory, antioxidant-rich diets tailored to your biology

Metabolic Health Optimization

Custom activity plans and glucose regulation strategies to support metabolic balance

Immune & Stress Resilience

Breathwork, circadian rhythm alignment, and targeted supplementation based on your immune profile

Gut & Microbiome Support

Insights-driven tracking to strengthen immune modulation through gut health

We Don’t Just Prevent We Empower

At TyMe, we view prevention not as a checklist—but as a continuous conversation between your biology, your lifestyle, and cutting-edge science.

Our role is to guide you—with purpose, precision, and compassion.

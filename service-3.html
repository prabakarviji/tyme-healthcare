TyMe Research Labs

A Platform for Precision Drug Development & Testing

About Research Labs

Research Labs is the translational drug development and preclinical manufacturing division of TyMe Institute, an integrated cancer prevention and therapeutic discovery institute. We specialize in early-stage manufacturing, drug bioactivity screening, and collaborative oncology innovation.

Our Mission

To accelerate the development of breakthrough therapies through agile manufacturing and precision biological testing, advancing new medicines from concept to clinic.

Contract Testing Services (CMO Model)

Fee-for-service testing for 3rd-party compounds.NCI-60 cancer cell panel for comparative potency profiling. Custom tumor panels, 3D-organoid models and organ-on-a-chip screening. Designed for biotech, pharma, and academic labs

High-Throughput Bioactivity Screening

In vitro screening in cancer cell lines, immune cells, and stem cell-derived. Assay platforms for cytotoxicity, immune activation, and mechanistic studies

Drug Development and Manufacturing

In silico drug design, target profiling, docking and optimization. R&D scale, small-batch synthesis of novel small molecule compounds. Flow chemistry and microreactor-enabled production. Rapid iteration for medicinal chemistry teams

Formulation & Process Development

Pre-formulation chemistry and delivery optimization. Solubility and stability screening. Drug loading and vehicle compatibility studies

Regulatory & IND Support

Early-stage CMC (Chemistry, Manufacturing, Controls) documentation. Pilot production for GLP-enabling studies

Why Partner with Research Labs

U.S.-Based Infrastructure

Modern lab facilities with scalable manufacturing capacity, built for responsiveness and compliance.

Oncology & Immunotherapy Expertise

Multi-disciplinary team with real-world experience across key therapeutic classes.

Fast Turnaround, Transparent Scope

Accelerated timelines with upfront planning and clear deliverables.

Built for Early-Stage Innovators

Ideal for startups, academic labs, and translational teams looking for an agile partner.

Preserve your biology today. Power your health tomorrow.

About BioVault

BioVault is the precision biobanking division of TyMe Institute, offering comprehensive biospecimen preservation and long-term storage for individuals, researchers, and future-forward therapeutic applications. Our infrastructure supports a fully integrated platform for cancer prevention, risk monitoring, and regenerative medicine.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and institutions by safeguarding high-quality biological material for future diagnostics, immune therapies, regenerative applications, and personalized medicine.

Comprehensive Biospecimen Banking

  • Long-term cryopreservation of blood, plasma, DNA, RNA, microbiome, and mitochondria
  • High-integrity sample processing using validated SOPs

DNA Archiving for Future Therapeutics

First biobank to preserve DNA explicitly for future applications in:

  • Personalized vaccines
  • Genomic reprogramming
  • Regenerative medicine

Microbiome & Mitochondrial Preservation

  • Targeted extraction and storage of gut microbiota and mitochondria
  • Future use in immune modulation, neuroregeneration, and metabolic restoration

Immune & Molecular Profiling (Optional Add-On)

  • Longitudinal immune biomarker tracking
  • Genomic and transcriptomic analysis integration with client records

Secure, HIPAA-Compliant Storage Infrastructure

  • Redundant cryostorage with 24/7 environmental monitoring
  • Encrypted digital inventory and sample traceability

Why Choose BioVault

Future-proof your biology for personalized medicine Preserve samples at peak health to support future interventions Ideal for health-conscious individuals, longevity-minded clients, and research- forward clinicians Integrated with early cancer detection and immune profiling programs

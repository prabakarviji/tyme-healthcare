About TyMe BioVault

TyMe BioVault is the precision biobanking division of TyMe Institute, offering comprehensive biospecimen preservation and long-term storage for individuals, researchers, and future-forward therapeutic applications. Our infrastructure supports a fully integrated platform for cancer prevention, risk monitoring, and regenerative medicine.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and institutions by safeguarding high-quality biological material for future diagnostics, immune therapies, regenerative applications, and personalized medicine.

Why Choose BioVault

  • Future-proof your biology for personalized medicine
  • Preserve samples at peak health to support future interventions
  • Ideal for health-conscious individuals, longevity-minded clients, and research
  • forward clinicians
  • Integrated with early cancer detection and immune profiling programs

Our Core Capabilities and Key Offerings

Comprehensive Biospecimen Banking

Long-term cryopreservation of blood, plasma, DNA, RNA, microbiome, and mitochondria.High-integrity sample processing using validated SOPs

Microbiome & Mitochondrial Preservation
